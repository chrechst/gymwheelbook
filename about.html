Cheyenne Rechsteiner is a two-times world champion in wheel gymnastics and founder of Wheel Gymnastics Club Liestal.


Cheyenne started wheel gymnastics in 2001 at the age of 7 in Birsfelden. Being inspired by the accomplishments of her teammate Cécile, she never missed a training session and progressed quickly. Her efforts lead to successful participations in Swiss Championships in 2005 and 2007 and a place on the national squad.


Being limited to only training straightline and vault in Birsfelden, she decided to switch gyms and train with national coaches Jasmin Braunwalder and Claudia Herger in Zurich. Under their guidance she participated in six World Championships, collecting a total of 15 medals, including two world championship titles, 5 silver and 8 bronze medals.


Following worlds in 2018 she announced her retirement to have more time for her biotechnology studies and growing love for coaching. With the help of long-time supporter Jocelyne Hänggi she founded a new wheel gymnastics club in Liestal, where she now serves as the head coach. Being encouraged by her gymnasts and coaches, she started training again and made her comeback at the 2019 Team World Cup in Japan.
