gymWHEEL launches


Jul 2, 2020 | Posted by: Cheyenne Rechsteiner


Welcome to gymWHEEL!


I'm Cheyenne, a 26 years old wheel gymnastics enthusiast. I compete internationally for Switzerland and am the head coach of Liestal Wheel Gymnastics Club.


The aim of this blog is to complement my upcoming wheel gymnastics book. It is the medium through which I will answer all your questions about the book or wheel gymnastics in general. Further, I intend to post articles on selected topics that did not find their way into the book, but I deem valuable nevertheless. Please feel free to contact me with questions, suggestions or your own contributions.


It's my pleasure to serve you. Let this adventure begin!
